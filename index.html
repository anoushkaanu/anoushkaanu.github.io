This application is developed to help the farmers in agriculture by suggesting them the suitable crops,fertilers and other information generally needed

The functions which we wish to perform through this website are

  • CROP IDENTIFICATION
    • In this section the farmer can give the details of soil,season,water availability to identify the best crop suitable.
  • CROP STAGES
    • In this section the farmers are provided with the information/stages of the crop which they wish to know about.
  • FERTILIZERS
    • THis section is to give the information about fertilizers to be used,the quantity and the frequency in which it should be used.
  • MARKET PRICES
    • In this section we have the past and current prices of the selected crop.
  • WATER USAGE
    • In this section farmers can be provided with the details about amount of water to be used and the frequency at which the crop is to be watered for the given soil type.
  • STORAGE
    • This section contains the information about the storage method and duration of the produce of the raised crop.
